Engram Collector!

Rules: You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Your job is to match that number by clicking on the assortment of engrams at the bottom. Each of which have been given a random hidden value. Clicking on an engram will add its value to the total score.

When the game restarts each of the crystals will have a new value.
